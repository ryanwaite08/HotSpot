Welcome to HotSpot!

This is a new and cool way to connect with people and display who you are!

Everyone has thier own SpotLight! You choose what you want to have in your SpotLight, in addition to the other posts you make. You can also make it to SpotLight of the month, depending on your LightShow! (Which is your account activity).

The intention of HotSpot is to better notify people of the things you do. If you're an Artist, Dancer, or anything else, you can give realtime updates to your followers! The best thing about HotSpot, is it's customizations! You can choose what kind of notifications to send and receive as a user, as well as customizing your profile to the extreme!
